    Set family certificates #

    This file defines the certificator of two families of sets. If we consider set families 𝒜 and ℬ as probabilistic events, the size of the certificator 𝒜 □ ℬ corresponds to the probability that 𝒜 and ℬ occur "disjointly".

    Main declarations #

    • finset.certificator: Certificator of two elements of a Boolean algebra
    • finset.card_certificator_le: The Van den Berg-Kesten-Reimer inequality: The probability that 𝒜 and ℬ occur "disjointly" is less than the product of their probabilities.

    References #

    • D. Reimer, Proof of the Van den Berg–Kesten Conjecture
